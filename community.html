MCHD Community

Our community is a mosaic of unique experiences, diverse lifestyles, and varied understandings of the broader world.

MCHD celebrates and nurtures individual differences and affirms sameness through a culture of inclusion, care, and respect.

Our perspective is constantly enriched and renewed by the involvement of families whose active presence, stories, and commitment help us all grow.

We are all united in our passion for peace as Citizens of the World.

Community Characteristics

Diversity at MCHD is not a number but a dynamic experience of rich interactions among our students, faculty, staff, and families in the classroom, on the playground, and at organized school events. This page offers a current snapshot of the unique atmosphere within which our students learn about each other, their community, and the world.
